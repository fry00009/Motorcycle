Motorcycle Awareness
Why Is It Important?

Hi, my name is Dakota, the author and creator of this site. In my opinion, riding a motorcycle is one of the best and most free feelings ever. I made this site to discuss the topic of motorcycle awareness and safety. The statistics shown thus far are both staggering and saddening. The data used for this site is also only taking into account America.

With this site I wanted to provide helpful tips and practices for both riders and drivers to keep riders safe.

Below are two clickable bubbles, one for riders and one for drivers. I encourage you to view both pages, both will have a link that can return you to this page, or the opposing page upon completion.

Thank you for taking the time to view my site.
